Portfolio Instructions

This portfolio is divided into 10 separate labs, each containing a 'Header Files' folder, 'Source Files' folder, and 'Data Files' folder.

All files in these folders should be included in IDE projects in order for them to compile correctly.  Make sure to put Data Files in the proper location, as these very from based on your IDE's requirements.

All .CPP files have been placed in their respective 'Source Files' directories,
all .H files in their respective 'Header Files' directories,
and all other files in their respective 'Data Files' directories.
